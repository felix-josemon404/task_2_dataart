Evolution of TV

1930

Cathode Ray Tube TV

Cathode ray was the first TV screen. It was huge and bulky, but revolutionary for its time.

1997

Flat Panel TV

LCD and plasma TVs brought slimmer designs and improved image quality.

2007

LED TV

LED technology made TVs thinner, brighter, and more energy-efficient.

2013

OLED TV

OLED displays offer deeper blacks, vibrant colors, and ultra-thin designs.
